About Us

JK Farm is an agricultural initiative based in Rwanda dedicated to promoting small-scale farming, kitchen gardens, organic fertilizer production, mushroom-based products, and empowering farmers through comprehensive training programs. We are committed to fostering sustainability, innovation, and food security in local communities.

Our Projects

Small-Scale Farming

Innovative approaches that help local farmers increase productivity and sustainability on small plots of land.

Kitchen Gardens

Promoting the creation of home gardens to enhance household nutrition and food independence.

Development and use of eco-friendly organic fertilizer that enriches soil health naturally.

Mushroom Products

Harnessing mushroom farming techniques to provide nutritious food and create economic opportunities.

Comprehensive training programs that equip farmers with skills for sustainable agriculture.
